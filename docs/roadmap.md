# Product Roadmap

## Phase 1 — MVP

- Web landing page.
- Explore/search.
- City guides.
- Place details.
- Safety badges.
- Mock booking request.
- Mobile app discovery flow.
- Admin review page.

## Phase 2 — Real backend

- Supabase auth.
- User profiles.
- Saved places.
- Trips and trip items.
- Reviews and reports.
- Admin approval workflow.

## Phase 3 — Partnerships

- Hotel affiliate API.
- Ticketmaster/Eventbrite feeds.
- Local queer venue partnerships.
- Tourism board sponsored city pages.
- Stripe checkout.

## Phase 4 — Marketplace

- Verified business listings.
- Paid promoted placements.
- Premium traveler membership.
- Concierge trip planning.
- Group trip planning.
- Push notifications for safety alerts and event changes.

## Phase 5 — Global trust layer

- Destination-level safety context.
- Traveler-specific recommendations.
- Local ambassador program.
- Incident reporting and moderation ops.
- Emergency contact and itinerary sharing.
